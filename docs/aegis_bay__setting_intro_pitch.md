**"A rainy city where every kind of story can begin."**

Aegis Bay is a highly integrated urban fantasy metropolis on the Pacific Northwest coast. Mythics, powered humans, and mundane humans share the same streets, schools, jobs, apartments, and bus routes. Coexistence is real. Equality is uneven. The city keeps going.

When the Veil collapsed here in the 1890s, Aegis Bay skipped the romantic phase of supernatural emergence and threw magic straight into the industrial era. Today it is the "Geneva of Mythic Policy" — a working city where many kinds of lives unfold in public.

**What to expect in Aegis Bay:**
* :cityscape: **Public coexistence:** Minotaurs work the docks, kitsune serve coffee, fae claim the streetlights. The vibe is contemporary, class-conscious, and anime-influenced — unmistakably urban.
* :card_box: **Strong civic bones:** The Department of Heroic Affairs regulates and classifies. The Mythic Tax shapes daily budgets. Private Heroic Contractors hold the response market. Friction is real and structural.
* :handshake: **Neighborhood life:** Unions, activists, school clubs, mutual-aid networks, mall hangouts, late-night diners, ferry crews. The city runs on people showing up for each other.

**A sandbox for many kinds of stories**
Every session begins on the **Route 42 Cross-City Bus**. The AI offers three story-launch stops tailored to your character — pick one, or write your own.

Lanes the setting naturally supports: slice-of-life, coming-of-age, romance, procedural drama, working-class grit, corporate heroics, occult mystery, nightlife, errand-day drift, noir, survival horror, high-society politics, and more.

Pick a stop. Bring an umbrella.
