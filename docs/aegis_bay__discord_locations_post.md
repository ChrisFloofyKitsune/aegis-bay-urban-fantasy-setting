# :round_pushpin: AEGIS BAY CITY GUIDE: CANON LOCATIONS

*The city splits into distinct districts. Each one supports a different texture of story. Feel free to invent your own corners; here are the canon anchors.*

* **The City Core:** Orbits the brutalist DHA Headquarters. Law firms, activist offices, government buildings, and 24-hour copy shops. *(Procedural drama, legal friction, civic politics.)*
* **The Docks & Industrial Waterfront:** Rust, chain-link, freight, magical materials, deep union history. *(Working-class grit, labor solidarity, street-level heroics.)*
* **Brentwood & The Enclaves:** Aging walkups, corner stores, mutual-aid fridges, dense mythic community hubs. *(Neighborhood life, family tension, local comedy, slice-of-life.)*
* **Cedar Mill (Suburbs):** Curving streets, strip malls, school parking lots. Integration looks clean here — sometimes isolating. *(Coming-of-age, family life, teen ensembles, school stories.)*
* **The Timberline (Outskirts):** Where subdivisions stop and the old-growth pines begin. DHA jurisdiction gets fuzzy. *(Cryptid hunting, weird woods, survival, folklore.)*
* **The University District:** Students, protests, coffee shops, theory, subculture, late-night conversations. *(Student life, activism, occult curiosity, friend groups, romance.)*
* **Old Town:** Cobblestones, heritage businesses, old bars, corners that feel closer to elsewhere. *(Occult mystery, history, local lore, dates.)*
* **The Convention Center:** Fluorescent purgatory hosting summits, expos, trade shows, and fan events. Neutral ground. *(Forced proximity, event chaos, diplomacy.)*
* **The Promenade (Commercial District):** Open-air mall, arcade light, teenage hangouts, date spots. Home to Lumina Heroics' glass HQ. *(Shopping, celebrity sightings, retail comedy, social drift.)*
* **The Transit Corridors:** Buses, trains, ferries — every social layer overlaps for a few damp minutes at a time. *(Coincidence, meet-cutes, stillness, routine.)*
