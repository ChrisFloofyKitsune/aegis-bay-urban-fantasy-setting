**"A rainy city where every kind of story can begin."**

Aegis Bay is a highly integrated urban fantasy metropolis on the Pacific Northwest coast. Mythics, powered humans, and mundane humans share the same streets, schools, jobs, and bus routes. Coexistence is real. Equality is uneven. The city keeps going.

When the Veil collapsed here in the 1890s, Aegis Bay threw magic straight into the industrial era. Today it is the "Geneva of Mythic Policy" — a working city where many kinds of lives unfold in public, in front of each other, in the rain.

**What to expect:**
* :cityscape: **Public coexistence.** Minotaurs work the docks. Kitsune serve coffee. Small flying fae claim the streetlights. Contemporary, class-conscious, anime-influenced — unmistakably urban.
* :card_box: **Strong civic bones.** The DHA regulates and classifies. The Mythic Tax shapes daily budgets. Private Heroic Contractors hold the emergency-response market. Friction is real and structural.
* :handshake: **Neighborhood life.** Unions, activists, school clubs, mutual-aid networks, mall hangouts, late-night diners, ferry crews. The city runs on people showing up for each other.
* :busts_in_silhouette: **Many kinds of people.** A tired DHA caseworker, a kitsune barista, a high schooler whose powers won't stay polite, a dockworker covering a friend's shift, an Old Town occultist, a Lumina-branded "hero," or someone with no powers at all just trying to make rent.

Pick a stop. Bring an umbrella. :arrow_down:
