# Aegis Bay — Urban Fantasy Setting

> A rainy city where every kind of story can begin.

**Aegis Bay** is a highly integrated urban fantasy setting on the Pacific Northwest coast, designed as a worldbuilding sandbox and an AI-ready world prompt. Mythics, powered humans, and mundane humans share the same streets, schools, workplaces, and bus routes. Coexistence is real. Equality is uneven. The city keeps going.

This repo holds the deployable world prompt, the design archive that shaped it, and the supporting documents.

## The setting at a glance

- A high-friction urban fantasy metropolis where the supernatural is public and bureaucratically managed.
- The Veil collapsed locally in the 1890s, throwing magic straight into the industrial era.
- The Department of Heroic Affairs (DHA) regulates, classifies, and dispatches. The Mythic Tax shapes daily budgets. Private Heroic Contractors hold the response market. Unions and activists push back.
- Geography dictates genre. Different districts naturally support different kinds of stories.
- Every story begins on the Route 42 Cross-City Bus in the rain.

For the full pitch, see [`docs/aegis_bay__setting_intro_pitch.md`](./docs/aegis_bay__setting_intro_pitch.md).

## Using the world prompt

1. Open [`world_prompt.md`](./world_prompt.md).
2. Paste it into your AI roleplay platform's world / system prompt field.
3. Load a Main Character and any companions.
4. The first response will put you on the Route 42 Cross-City Bus and offer three story-launch stops tailored to your character.
