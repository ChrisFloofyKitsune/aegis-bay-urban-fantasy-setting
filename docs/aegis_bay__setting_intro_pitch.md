# Welcome to Aegis Bay (Circa 2026)

**"Magic is real, the city is tired, and your stop is coming up."**

> Working draft for the human-facing setting intro / pitch.

## Setting Pitch

Aegis Bay is a high-friction urban fantasy metropolis on the rainy Pacific Northwest coast. This is not a secret-world masquerade, and it is not a whimsical fairy tale. When the Veil collapsed here in the 1890s, the city skipped the romantic phase of supernatural history and threw magic straight into the Industrial Revolution.

Today, Aegis Bay is the "Geneva of Mythic Policy"—a heavily regulated, hyper-capitalist city where everyday coexistence grinds against the crushing weight of the Department of Heroic Affairs (DHA).

## What to Expect in Aegis Bay

- :cityscape: **Seamless (but tired) integration:** Minotaurs work the docks, kitsune serve coffee, and small flying fae get treated like city pigeons. The vibe is contemporary, class-conscious, and anime-influenced without losing its urban grit.
- :card_box: **Bureaucracy as physics:** The main antagonist is not a Dark Lord. It is the DHA, the Mythic Tax, registration tiers, permit hell, and heavily armed Private Heroic Contractors deciding which emergencies are worth responding to.
- :handshake: **Neighborhood solidarity:** The city survives because neighbors cover each other’s shifts, unions protect their own, activists fight uphill battles, and local block watches do what the institutions won’t.

## Where Stories Begin

Every journey starts on the **Route 42 Cross-City Bus**. From there, the AI offers three possible stops—three different ways your story could begin—each tailored to your character. You can pick one, or write your own.

## Current Story Lanes

- **Slice-of-Life** (Cedar Mill or Brentwood) - Head home, help a neighbor, show up for a community event, or deal with quiet personal drama.
- **Coming-of-Age / Student Life** (Cedar Mill, Brentwood, or the University District) - Meet up with friends, survive school politics, or deal with the messy reality of growing up Mythic.
- **Procedural / Political Drama** (The City Core) - Face DHA audits, legal trouble, policy fights, or the machinery of the city itself.
- **Working-Class Grit** (The Docks) - Pull a brutal shift, back a coworker, move dangerous cargo, or stand the line with the union.
- **Corporate Security / PHC** (Convention Center or Promenade) - Handle a VIP contract, a PR disaster, or sanctioned hero work under corporate rules.
- **Active Dispatch / Emergency Response** (Any Location) - Get the call and deploy immediately into a live containment breach, civic emergency, or unfolding disaster.
- **Occult Investigation** (Old Town or the Timberline) - Follow a rumor, investigate a thin place, or dig into something the city would rather leave unexplained.
- **Street-Level Conflict** (Transit Intersection) - Get caught in a barricade, protest, riot, or sudden public incident that forces immediate choices.
- **Academic / Activism** (University District) - Stand with student organizers, MRC activists, or theorists poking at truths the DHA wants buried.
- **Noir / Underworld** (The Rust Yards) - Meet informants, chase smugglers, find off-the-books help, or make a deal you probably shouldn’t.
- **Survival / Wild Mythic** (The Timberline) - Track a cryptid, repair broken wards, or step too far past the edge of the city.
- **High-Society / Political** (The Core Penthouses) - Crash a gala, navigate elite circles, or get close to the people who profit most from the system.

Aegis Bay is a city where the seams show.
